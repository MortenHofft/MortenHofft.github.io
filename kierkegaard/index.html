Kierkegaard

Ten danish ebooks

By Morten Høfft - February 20, 2014
  • Home
  • About

The Søren Kierkegaard Research Center has kindly published the collected works of Kierkegaard online. Unfortunately they are not made available as e-books, but as XML. The notation kn1 is thoroughly documented. I have made a parser that generates e-books based on the XML. Scholar comments are not included as I have not been able to get in contact with the Danish Royal Library who holds the rights to the digital material. The original text however should be fine to share with anyone who cares about Kierkegaard. It is all in Danish and so is the remainder of the post.

Materialet er primært lavet til mig selv og derfor ikke testet i et bredt spektrum af læsere. Det er lavet med Apples iBooks i sinde, men er også testet på Bluefire og NeoSoar. De to sidstnævnte synes dog at være langsomme og have problemer med at vise indholdsfortegnelsen det rette sted. Men læsbart er det bestemt i alle tre. Kindles eget format (.mobi) har jeg kun testet på computeren da jeg ikke ejer en Kindle. Men det så ud til at fungere glimrende. Der er ingen forside, så den genereres automatisk af ebogs-læseren.
