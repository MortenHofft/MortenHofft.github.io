I am a software developer, specializing in machine learning and computer vision. Besides my master I have a bachelor in philosophy and a bachelor in architecture. Say Hello.

This site

This site works as documentation and motivation to complete undertakings, which – in my experience – all to often end up half-baked and eventually lost. It is primarily for learning and the joy of it, but some projects might turn out to have the interest of other people.

Design and implementation

I tried to keep things simple and spent little time on frameworks and design. It seemed overkill to spent time on a beautiful production line when I - as of writing this - have no content. I decision I might regret if I get around to producing some content.

The blue color is lifted of Coderwall. The navigation bar is inspired by the Cicero blog theme. The header by the magazine Nautilus.
